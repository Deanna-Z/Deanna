About Me

Deanna is an aspiring Technical Artist seeking to bridge creativity and technology in game design, with a focus on 3D modeling, animation, and visual effects using tools like Unity and Unreal Engine.

With over eight years of experience in civil engineering, Deanna brings a strong background in structural design, project management, and cross-functional collaboration. Now transitioning into the game design industry, she combines her problem-solving and data visualization skills with recent expertise in 3D modeling and animation. Her goal is to create immersive, visually compelling experiences that captivate players while ensuring technical precision.
